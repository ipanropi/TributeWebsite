Facts About Alan Turing

  • Alan Turing is the father of modern computer science.
  • Alan Turing played a key role in winning World War II.
  • Alan Turing broke the rules to write to Winston Churchill.
  • Alan Turing had some odd habits.
  • Alan Turing rode his bike 62 miles to get to the first day of school.
  • Alan Turing tried out for the Olympics.
  • Alan Turing was prosecuted for being gay.
  • The British government only recently apologized for Alan Turing's conviction.
